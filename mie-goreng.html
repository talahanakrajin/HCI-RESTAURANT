Mie Goreng
A street food legend, dances upon your taste buds. Imagine springy noodles, wok-tossed to perfection, adorned with a sweet and savory symphony—the secret lies in Kecap Manis, that syrupy elixir of sweet soy sauce. Within this tangled embrace, you’ll find tender chicken, succulent prawns, and a vegetable medley—each bite a revelation. And behold, the delicate egg ribbons, like golden threads woven by culinary artisans, add elegance to this dish. Mie Goreng: where tradition meets innovation, and every forkful whispers tales of Indonesian spice markets

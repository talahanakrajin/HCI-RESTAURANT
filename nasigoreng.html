Pineapple Fried Rice

Rp. 999.600,00

Amidst the sizzle of a bustling kitchen, where woks pirouette over open flames, emerges a symphony of flavors: Pineapple Fried Rice. Imagine golden grains of jasmine rice, each kissed by the sun, cradling vibrant chunks of pineapple—sweetness entwined with a hint of tropical mischief. The chef, an alchemist of the culinary realm, orchestrates this dish with precision. First, fragrant garlic and shallots perform their aromatic ballet, coaxing forth secrets from the rice. Then, a dash of soy sauce pirouettes across the pan, infusing every grain with umami. But the pièce de résistance: pineapple. Its juicy rebellion against convention—tangy, yet harmonious—melds seamlessly with the rice. Cashews add a delicate crunch, while plump raisins whisper tales of distant shores. Garnished with cilantro and a sprinkle of optimism, this creation transcends mere sustenance. It’s a passport to paradise, a taste of sunshine on a plate.

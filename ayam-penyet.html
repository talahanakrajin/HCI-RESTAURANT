We serve the Best chicken in town
Ayam Penyet
Born in the bustling kitchens of East Java, is a symphony of textures and spices. Imagine succulent fried chicken, marinated in turmeric, lemongrass, and galangal. But the pièce de résistance? The sambal—a fiery blend of chilies, anchovies, and tamarind—smashed into a paste. Each bite reveals tender meat, crispy skin, and a dance of flavors. Served with cucumber, fried tofu, and tempeh, Ayam Penyet is a tribute to Surabaya’s vibrant street food scene. Dive in, dear diner, and let the equatorial breeze carry you to Indonesia’s heart.

Soto Bandung
Soto Bandung is a beloved Indonesian soup originating from the city of Bandung in West Java. This flavorful dish is known for its rich, aromatic broth, typically made from beef or chicken, infused with spices like lemongrass, ginger, and turmeric. The soup is often garnished with fresh vegetables, such as bean sprouts, green onions, and sometimes fried shallots, adding a delightful crunch.

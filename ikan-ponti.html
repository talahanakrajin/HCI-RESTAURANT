Ikan Ponti
Like a river’s whispered secret, embodies the essence of Borneo’s waters. Picture succulent fish—freshly caught—marinated in a symphony of spices: turmeric, lemongrass, and galangal. The grill sings its sizzling ode, charring the fish to perfection. But the pièce de résistance? The tangy tamarind glaze, a burst of tropical sunshine. Each forkful transports you to the bustling markets of Pontianak, where flavors dance along the Kapuas River. Dive in, dear diner, and let the equatorial breeze carry you away.
